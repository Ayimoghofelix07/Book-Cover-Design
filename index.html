Just A Simple Story Design By Felix

The young Achievers series is a set of books designed to introduce children to the wonderful world of education. The books are filled with engaging activities that help children understand the basic principles of different subjects and gain a strong foothold in the vast world of learning.
The Social Studies books help children understand the norms and traditions of the society they live and learn good habits and manners in order to become an ideal member of the society. The concepts are taught with relevant examples and fun activities to help the children understand them better
